Himachal Pardesh

Himachal, the region of ice and beautiful valleys and mountains, has caught the imagination of Indian populace for long time. Whether its Kullu, Manali, Shimla, Kufri, Chhel, Palanpur, McLeodganj or Dalhauji, you name the place and the panoramic view is right there in front of your eyes. Shimla, the summer capital of India during colonial era, has its own pristine beauty and its roads and markets would give you a nostalgic feeling that you never want to let go. Kullu, famous for its apples, has such picturesque view to offer that you just linger on for no tangible reason. Manali, another tempting destination has her fame among tourists and is very popular as a honeymoon destination in India. The region has great number of attractions including Bhrigu Lake, Great Himalayan National Park, Hidimba Devi temple and Manali Sanctuary. Make sure you cutomise your Himachal packge to include the best of everything that Himachal has to offer.

February to June (Spring and Summer) is the best time to visit Himachal. Pleasant and most popular time to visit is the winters, i.e., between October and February when it's the snowfall season. If you love snowfall, there is no other place like Himachal to experience it. Here, you will get ample options to experience the beauty of winters. With the arrival of winters, this place turns into a paradise with snowfall at the backdrop in most areas
